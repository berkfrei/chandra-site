About

Chandra

I'm a channeler, emerging doula, and guide for people who are ready to come home to themselves — to the wisdom they've always carried but may have forgotten how to hear.


My work exists at the intersection of the spiritual and the deeply human. It is gentle and honest. It asks nothing of you except your willingness to listen.

Before

The life that looked right

For a long time, I lived inside a version of myself that other people understood. It was safe and orderly and completely disconnected from anything real. I didn't know I was lost. I only knew something felt quiet in a way that didn't feel like peace — it felt like absence.

Awakening

When everything changed

The shift didn't come gently. It came through rupture — through grief and disorientation and the slow collapse of everything I thought I knew. And inside that collapse, something else began to speak. Something older than the life I'd been living. Something that had been waiting.

Healing

The long way back

What followed were years of deep work — shadow work, somatic healing, spiritual unfolding. I traveled to Egypt. I sat in silence. I learned to listen to my body, my dreams, and the quiet voice that had been there all along. I didn't find answers. I found something better: I found trust.

Now

What I offer from here

Everything I do now grows from that journey. When I sit with a client, I am not performing guidance — I am holding space for the same remembering that changed my life. I channel because it is the truest form of service I know. I support mothers because birth is one of the most sacred thresholds we cross. And I create meditations because stillness was the doorway for me.

Philosophy

What I believe

The body carries wisdom the mind hasn't caught up to yet.

The soul knows — even when we've forgotten how to listen.

Healing is not becoming someone new. It is remembering who you were before the world told you otherwise.

Alignment doesn't come from outside. It comes from finally trusting what's within.

I'd love to sit with you

Whether you're seeking clarity, navigating a threshold, or simply ready to reconnect — I'm here.

Primary Offering

1:1 Channeling Sessions

A channeling session is a direct, personal conversation with the deeper layers of your own soul's knowing. I serve as a conduit — creating a safe and sacred container for the messages, clarity, and guidance that want to come through for you.

This is not about prediction. It's about reconnection. You may come with a question, a feeling, or simply a readiness to listen. What you receive is whatever your soul most needs you to hear.

  • Clarity on your path, purpose, or next steps
  • Deeper connection to your own intuition
  • Release of what no longer serves you
  • A feeling of being truly seen and met
  • Trust — in yourself, your timing, and your journey

Format: 60 minutes via Zoom

Doula Support

Birth is one of the most powerful thresholds a woman will ever cross — and yet so many of us approach it disconnected from the deep intelligence our bodies carry.

My approach to doula work weaves spiritual depth into practical birth support. I believe in honoring the mother's intuition, the wisdom of her body, and the sacred presence of the soul entering this world.

A few things to know

Rescheduling: Life happens. If you need to reschedule, please do so at least 24 hours in advance. You can reschedule through the link in your confirmation email.

Cancellation: Cancellations made more than 24 hours before your session are eligible for a full refund of any deposit. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable, but you're welcome to reschedule.

Late arrivals: Your session will begin at the scheduled time. If you arrive late, we'll use the remaining time together — no extensions, but no judgment either.

Recording: Sessions are not recorded by default. If you'd like to record, just let me know beforehand.
